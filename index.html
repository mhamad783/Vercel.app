Frequently Asked Questions

Is this safe to use?

Yes! Your token is processed securely and never stored. The automation follows Discord's API exactly as a regular client would.

Will I get banned?

We use the same API calls as Discord's official client. However, use at your own discretion as automation violates Discord's ToS.

What quests are supported?

Video quests, activity quests, and most standard quest types. Desktop-only quests may require manual completion.

How long does it take?

Most quests complete in under 2 minutes. Video quests may take slightly longer depending on the required watch time.
